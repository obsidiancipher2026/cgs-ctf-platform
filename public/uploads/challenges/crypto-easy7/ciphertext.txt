ROT47 Encrypted Message
=======================

Another rotation cipher, but this one rotates more than just letters.
It shifts the entire printable ASCII range (33-126).

Ciphertext:
rv$LC_Ecf08_bD0AcDE0=bEEbCDN

Hint: Unlike ROT13 (which only affects A-Za-z), ROT47 rotates all
printable characters from ! (33) through ~ (126) by 47 positions.
